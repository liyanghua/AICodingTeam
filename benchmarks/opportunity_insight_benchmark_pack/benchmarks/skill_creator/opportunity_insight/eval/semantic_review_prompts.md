# Semantic Review Prompts

## Scenario Fit
Does the generated Skill Pack clearly understand ecommerce opportunity insight as a business decision task, rather than a generic report generation task?

## Data Contract Quality
Does the Skill define required datasets, required fields, field meanings, quality rules, missing-field behavior and sample-data assumptions?

## Knowledge Contract Quality
Does the Skill include concrete ecommerce business claims, negative cases and strategy rules? Does it avoid generic marketing language?

## Output Usefulness
Would an ecommerce operator be able to use the generated opportunity cards in a weekly operations meeting?

## RSI Feedback
Does the Skill define what feedback to collect and which asset should be updated when the Skill fails?
